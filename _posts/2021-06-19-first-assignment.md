[Nintendo has begun their Electronic Entertainment Experience presentation](https://www.nintendo.com/e3/) with outlining the event being centered around new software for the Nintendo Switch.

Shinya Takahashi and Yoshiaki Koizumi, both game developers under Nintendo, opened the E3 presentation by giving an overview what should be happening throughout the live event.

A presentation of the newest games should follow after the introductions.
